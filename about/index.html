About PawTails

Built from the love hidden inside everyday routines.

The walks, meals, refilled bowls, and quiet check-ins are small on their own. Together, they are how a household says, “you are cared for.”

It started with Frida

A personal project for care that belongs to everyone.

PawTails began as a personal project inspired by Frida, a rottweiler who quickly became the center of the daily routine at home.

The app is designed for households where dog care is shared between partners, family, roommates, or sitters. Instead of relying on memory or scattered messages, PawTails gives everyone one place to see what has been done and what still needs care.
