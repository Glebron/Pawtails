About us

PawTails is built for people who care for pets together. We make daily routines clear, shared, and easy to follow, so no task gets missed and every pet gets consistent care.

What We Believe

  • Pet care should feel calm, not chaotic.
  • Families and caregivers should always stay in sync.
  • Simple tools can make routines more reliable every day.

What PawTails Helps With

  • Building daily and weekly routines for each pet.
  • Tracking completed tasks and maintaining healthy habits.
  • Sharing responsibilities with partners, family, and caregivers.
  • Keeping reminders and updates in one place.
