Privacy policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

PawTails (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights when you use the PawTails mobile app and related website (the “Service”).

1. Information We Collect

a) Information you provide

  • Account information, such as your email address and display name.
  • Pet profile details, such as pet name, traits, and routine preferences.
  • Household details, such as household name and member display names.
  • Content you create in the Service, including tasks, reminders, and activity or streak data.
  • Information you send when contacting support.

b) Information collected automatically

  • Device and app diagnostics, such as app version, device type, and crash logs.
  • Usage data related to feature performance and reliability.

c) Invite and sharing data

  • Invite link and QR-related data required to let household members join shared households.

2. How We Use Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide core app features (to-dos, calendar, streaks, household sharing).
  • Sync and display your data across sessions and devices.
  • Improve reliability, performance, and user experience.
  • Prevent abuse and protect app security.
  • Respond to support requests.

3. Legal Bases (for EEA/UK users)

If GDPR applies, we process data based on:

  • Performance of a contract (to provide the Service).
  • Legitimate interests (security, improvements, analytics).
  • Consent (where required).
  • Legal obligations (where required by law).

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service providers (hosting, analytics, crash reporting, customer support).
  • Other household members (only data necessary for shared household features).
  • Authorities if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

5. Data Retention

We retain data as long as needed to provide the Service and for legitimate business or legal purposes. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time (see Contact section).

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your data.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Delete your data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Receive a portable copy of your data.
  • Withdraw consent (where processing is based on consent).

To exercise your rights, contact us at pawtails@proton.me.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13 (or older age where required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without appropriate authorization.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect personal data. No system is 100% secure, but we continuously work to protect your information.

9. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for cross-border data transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new “Last updated” date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions or requests regarding privacy, contact:

  • Email: pawtails@proton.me
