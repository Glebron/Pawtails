Privacy policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information PawTails collects, how it is used, and what choices you have. By using PawTails, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account details such as email address and basic profile information.
  • Pet profile details you choose to add, including names, routines, tasks, and reminders.
  • Collaboration data when you invite family members or caregivers.
  • Technical and diagnostic data such as app version, crash logs, and performance metrics.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide core app functionality, including syncing and reminders.
  • To improve reliability, stability, and user experience.
  • To support collaboration features across shared pet profiles.
  • To respond to support requests and account-related questions.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with trusted service providers (for example hosting, database, and analytics providers) only as needed to operate PawTails.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information while your account is active, or as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

5. Your Choices

  • You can review and update profile and pet information in the app.
  • You can request account and data deletion by contacting us.
  • You can manage notifications in your device and app settings.

6. Children's Privacy

PawTails is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact pawtails@proton.me.
