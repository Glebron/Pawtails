Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PetCare collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

  • Device account ID used to keep app data linked to your device.
  • Pet profile and routine data including reminders and activity history.
  • Optional pet photo data if you upload profile images.
  • Collaboration data for invited pet members and access roles.
  • Push notification token and technical metadata.
  • Diagnostics and analytics via Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics.

How We Use Information

  • Provide pet routines, reminders, onboarding, and shared access.
  • Sync data across devices with Firebase Firestore.
  • Maintain reliability and improve app performance.

Data Sharing

  • Shared with pet members you invite to your workspace.
  • Processed by Firebase services acting as infrastructure providers.
  • Personal data is not sold.

Data Deletion and Retention

  • Data is retained while necessary for app functionality.
  • Reset and deletion flows remove local and related cloud records where applicable.
