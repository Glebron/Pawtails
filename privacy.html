Privacy policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

We collect only the information needed to run PawTails features, including pet profile data, routine tasks, reminders, collaboration details, and diagnostic logs.

Data is used to provide syncing, reminders, app reliability, and shared pet care access.

We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by infrastructure providers such as Firebase for app functionality.

To request data deletion or ask a privacy question, contact pawtails@proton.me.
