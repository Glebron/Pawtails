What the app does

Smart routines, shared with family.

PawTails helps people maintain healthy pet habits without chaos. These sections make the product easy to understand for visitors and App Review.

Routine Builder

Create day-by-day pet schedules and track completion history for feeding, walks, and care tasks.

Meal Planning

Store feeding plans and reminders so each pet gets the right amount at the right time.

Hydration & Health

Monitor daily water and optional health notes as part of a complete routine.

Collaboration

Invite family, partner, or caregiver to the same pet profile with role-based access.

Reliable sync

Cloud sync and notifications keep everyone up to date, even across multiple devices.
